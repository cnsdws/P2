    Password Generator

    This website will generate a unique password for use in various contexts. It does so by randomly generating and connecting words that will be easy for you to remember but hard for computers to crack or people to guess. It will give you a very secure password.

    Since some sites have criteria that require numbers or special characters, those can be added as options.

    How many words in the password? (enter a whole number between 2 and 9)  
    Include a number?  
    Include a special symbol, such as @?  
    CamelCase the words (that is make the first character capitalized)?  
    Include a dash between words?
